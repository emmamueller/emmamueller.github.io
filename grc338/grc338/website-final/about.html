Welcome to Fino's Mediterranean Grill, where we bring the vibrant flavors and rich traditions of the Mediterranean straight to your table. Our passion for fresh, high-quality ingredients is at the heart of everything we do, from our hand-crafted hummus to our perfectly grilled skewers. Whether you're craving tender lamb, flavorful seafood, or crisp, seasonal vegetables, we offer a variety of dishes that are both authentic and satisfying. We believe in creating a warm and inviting atmosphere, where every meal is an experience, and every bite tells a story. Join us and enjoy the best of Mediterranean cuisine, made with love and served with a smile.

The story of Fino's began with a simple yet powerful idea: to share the vibrant, flavorful cuisine of the Mediterranean with our community. The founders, who grew up in families that valued homemade, fresh meals, spent their childhoods surrounded by the smells of sizzling grills, aromatic spices, and fresh vegetables. Inspired by their love of food and the memories of family gatherings, they decided to bring these cherished flavors to the heart of the city.

With a passion for authentic Mediterranean recipes passed down through generations, they set out to create a place where people could experience the tastes of the Mediterranean in a welcoming, relaxed environment. They spent years perfecting their menu, sourcing the finest ingredients, and learning from chefs who had dedicated their lives to the craft. Their goal was simple: to create a restaurant that not only served delicious food but also brought people together, much like the Mediterranean cultures they celebrated.

From the very first day they opened their doors, Mediterranean Grill became more than just a restaurant—it became a gathering place. Locals quickly embraced the inviting atmosphere and the authentic, homemade flavors that filled every dish. The restaurant’s success grew, but its roots remained grounded in tradition, family, and a commitment to offering the best of Mediterranean cuisine. Today, we continue to honor those beginnings, always striving to deliver a memorable dining experience that celebrates the rich history and culture of the Mediterranean.
